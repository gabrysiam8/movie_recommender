Song recommender

This app is a project for Python in the Enterprise university course. It allows the user to see a list of song recommendations based on his mood.

Technologies

We used: Python, Django, Heroku (deployment)

How to use our app?

Simply choose up to 5 words describing your mood from the panel or search for the particular word in the search bar. Then push "Check your songs!" button et voila! Now enjoy your music.
